# Proof of Claim Checklist

- Debtor name, case number, court, chapter
- Bar date and notice source
- Creditor legal name and address
- Claim basis and amount
- Secured/unsecured/priority classification
- Collateral description and lien documents
- Supporting contracts, invoices, judgments, shipment records
- Interest, fees, costs, currency conversion
- Signature authority
- Filing confirmation and docket proof
